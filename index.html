Online Reviews SEO tips

Below are 3 tips for SEO. In the world of online reviews, there are lots of locations to gather those reviews: BBB, Yellow Pages, Yelp, etc. However, no matter what industry you are in, Google reviews are fast becoming king. The more Google reviews you can have mapping back to your website, the better off you will be.

  • Google reviews are king
  • Map review sites to your website
  • A large quantity of 4.9 star reviews is better than just a few 5 star reviews
